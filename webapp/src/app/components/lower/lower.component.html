Lower

90 minute training

15 April 2019 - 31 May 2019

Warming up

5 different sets in a row - 5 minutes

Warm up your upper body with low weights and a standing position. Do Rear Delt Raises, Side Lateral Raises, Standing Shoulder Press, Biceps Curls, Overhead Tricep Extensions.

Pull ups & Dips

2 supersets - 5 minutes

Pull up form: shoulder-width grip, pull your elbows to the floor & go all the way. Dip form: straighten your arms, dip below your elbows & lock your elbows at top.

Mat workouts

10 short sets - 10 minutes

10 different mat workout in 45 sec with 15 sec rest in between.

Horizontal Cable Woodchop

3 sets - 5 minutes

Do the Incline Dumbbell Press at 80% of your max. When all reps are done continue with the Dumbbell Squeeze Press on 60% of your max.

Ab Machine

3 sets - 5 minutes

Do the Cable Fly at 80% of your max. When all reps are done continue with the Low Cable Crossover.

Squats

5 sets with drop - 15 minutes

Key workout, focus on weight & drop the last set.

Leg press & Calve Press

3 supersets - 10 minutes

Do the Leg press at 80% of your max and the Calve Press on 60%.

Deadlift & Dumbbell Lunge

3 supersets - 15 minutes

Key workout, focus on weight. after deadlift, kill it with low weight dumbbell lunges until broken.

Shoulder press

5 different sets in a row - 10 minutes

Key workout, focus on weight.

Shoulder Hypertrophy

infinite sets - until no time is left

Hypertrophy, repeat low weight Dumbbell Lateral Raise, Front Dumbbell Raise & Standing Shoulder Press untill broken.
